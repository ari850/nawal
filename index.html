How it works

Built to remove the weakest link in digital security.

Instead of asking people to create and manage passwords, NawalKey verifies identity through devices users already trust, like their phone and laptop. That means no password databases, no credential reuse, and far less opportunity for attackers.

01

Trusted devices

Your identity is confirmed using devices you already own, creating a more natural and secure login experience.

02

No stored passwords

By removing reusable credentials, NawalKey reduces phishing risk, credential stuffing, and large-scale account compromise.

03

Secure recovery

If a device is lost, secure recovery options help users regain access without going back to weak password-based systems.

FAQ

Questions users would ask first.

These are the basic questions a customer or decision-maker would want answered right away.

What is NawalKey?

NawalKey is a passwordless identity system that uses trusted devices instead of passwords to verify users securely.

Who is it for?

NawalKey is designed for both everyday users and organizations that want a simpler, safer way to log in.

Do I still need passwords?

No. NawalKey is designed to eliminate passwords entirely for supported accounts and services.

What if I lose a device?

Secure recovery options allow you to regain access safely without falling back on vulnerable password-based methods.
