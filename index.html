FAQ

Questions people ask first

What is NawalKey?

NawalKey is a passwordless identity system that uses trusted devices instead of passwords to verify users securely.

Who is it for?

NawalKey is designed for both individuals and organizations that want a safer, simpler way to log in.

Do I still need passwords?

No. NawalKey is designed to eliminate passwords entirely for supported services.

What if I lose a device?

Secure recovery options allow you to regain access safely without returning to weak password-based methods.
